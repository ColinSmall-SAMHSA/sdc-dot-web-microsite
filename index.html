What is Secure Data Commons Platform?

The Secure Data Commons (SDC) is a proof of concept online data warehousing and analysis platform for transportation researchers. On this portal, researchers can take advantage of pre-established programming environments to access and analyze a growing set of transportation-related data sets.

The SDC platform is being developed as a collaborative environment for traffic engineers, researchers, data scientists, and anyone who is interested in carrying out research and analysis on different datasets related to traffic, weather, crashes, and others.

What Controls are Afforded a Data Provider?

  • The Secure Data Commons allows data providers to upload data from their projects in near-real-time and in an automated fashion
  • Raw data ingested within the system cannot be copied or exported
  • Data providers have complete control over who can access their data and metadata
  • Exports or copies of derived analysis results or datasets must be authorized by the data provider prior to being removed from the SDC
